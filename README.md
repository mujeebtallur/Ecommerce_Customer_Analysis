# Ecommerce_Customer_Analysis

A complete data analysis project to understand customer behavior, sales patterns, and business insights.

This project focuses on analyzing an e-commerce customer dataset to uncover key insights about sales performance, customer demographics, product demand, and order behavior.
The goal is to help businesses make data-driven decisions in marketing, inventory planning, and customer retention.

Objectives

✔️ Understand customer purchase behavior
✔️ Identify top-selling products & categories
✔️ Analyze revenue trends
✔️ Study customer segmentation patterns
✔️ Provide actionable insights for business growth
